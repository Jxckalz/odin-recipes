Cookies and Cream Milkshake

Description

This Oreo milkshake is easy to make, super kid-friendly, and delicious — a favorite on a hot day!

Ingredients

  • 6 Chocolate Sandwich Cookies (Ex. Oreo Cookies)
  • 3 Scoops of Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 3/4 Cup of Milk

Steps

  1. Blend chocolate sandwich cookies, ice cream, and milk together in a blender until smooth. Pour into 2 glasses.
